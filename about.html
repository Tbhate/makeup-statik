ME

Luxury Bridal Makeup Artist Mallorca | Makeup Artist & Hair Stylist

I am a makeup artist originally from Ukraine, with over 12 years of experience in the beauty industry. I work as a Bridal Makeup Artist in Mallorca, as well as a makeup artist and hair stylist for luxury events, photoshoots, and special occasions.

01

For more than 10 years, I have been working closely with brides in Mallorca and internationally, creating refined, elegant bridal looks for destination weddings. I have been part of many weddings throughout my career, and each one is a unique and meaningful experience. Weddings hold a very special place in my heart, and I truly love the atmosphere and emotions of the bridal day.

My professional journey began over 12 years ago with my first professional beauty courses, followed by extensive online and offline education with leading makeup artists worldwide. In addition, I graduated from an art school, where I developed a strong understanding of color harmony, light, shadow, shapes, and proportions. This artistic background gives me a significant advantage as a makeup artist and allows me to create balanced, sophisticated, and visually refined looks.

02

I offer a highly personalized approach to every client. Whether it is a bridal look, a luxury event, or a professional photoshoot, I carefully adapt makeup and hair styling to enhance natural beauty and suit individual features. From soft, elegant luxury nude makeup to bold, glamorous, or fashion-forward styles — I can confidently create looks of any complexity, always with attention to detail, longevity, and comfort.

One of my key strengths is working fast while maintaining high-end quality. Years of experience allow me to work efficiently, calmly, and professionally — an essential quality for bridal mornings, events, and time-sensitive shoots.

I am punctual, responsible, and deeply passionate about working with women and highlighting their beauty. A bridal morning is not only about makeup and hair — it is also about trust, emotional support, and a peaceful atmosphere. I know how to calm, support, and create a sense of ease, ensuring you feel confident and relaxed.

My goal is for you to feel 100% confident, radiant, and luxurious — whether you are a bride, a guest at an exclusive event, or in front of the camera at a photoshoot in Mallorca.
